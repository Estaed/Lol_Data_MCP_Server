<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span></h2>
<p><i>Only the boldest of men and women dare take the risky trip to the hottest spot in <a href="/en-us/Valoran" class="mw-redirect" title="Valoran">Valoran</a>, in the very center of the <a href="/en-us/Shurima" class="mw-redirect" title="Shurima">Shurima</a> Desert. Those who successfully make the journey may bathe their swords in a magical pool of the purest water known to this world. Any edge dipped into this pool will stay sharper and strike truer than any other sword.</i></p><div style="clear:left"></div>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Infinity Edge</b> shares its name with a set of Infinity Weapons from the game <i><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_II" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lineage II">Lineage II</a>,</i> which are the strongest weapons available. <b>Infinity Edge</b>&#160;bears similarities to the <a target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://l2vn.com/upload/images/InfinityShaper.png">Infinity Shaper</a>.</li>
<li>As hinted in its lore, the Infinity Edge got its powers from the <i>Oasis of the Dawn</i> located in the Ancient <a href="/en-us/Shurima" class="mw-redirect" title="Shurima">Shuriman</a> Capital, the once center of the vast Shuriman Empire. The Oasis is described as a pool with many magical properties.</li>
