Abilities targeted at allied champions signal <i>Aery</i> to dash to them, shielding them for <span class="pp-tooltip" style="position:relative; border-bottom:1px dotted; cursor:help;" data-bot_values="30;33;36;39;42;45;48;51;54;56;59;62;65;68;71;74;77;80" data-top_values="">30 – 80 (based on level)</span> <span style="color: #7A6DFF; white-space:normal">(+ 25% AP)</span> <span style="color:orange; white-space:normal">(+ 40% <b>bonus</b> AD)</span> for 2 seconds.</li>
<li><i>Aery</i> lingers on the target for 2 second before flying back to you, and cannot be sent out again until she returns to you. <i>Aery is initially very slow, but gradually accelerates, and can be picked up by moving near her.</i></li>
<li><span class="template_sbc"><b>Adaptive:</b></span> Deals either physical or magic depending on which would deal the least damage.</li></ul></li></ul></div>

<b>Sight</b> is a property of <a href="/en-us/Unit" class="mw-disambig" title="Unit">units</a>, <a href="/en-us/Structures" class="mw-redirect" title="Structures">structures</a>, certain <a href="/en-us/Summoner_spell" title="Summoner spell">summoner spells</a>, <a href="/en-us/Item" title="Item">items</a>, and <a href="/en-us/Champion_ability" title="Champion ability">abilities</a> that represents a team possessing vision of a target area or unit. It is always <span class="glossary" style="white-space:pre; position:relative;" data-game="lol" data-tip="Center range"><span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/en-us/Range" title="Range"><img src="/en-us/images/thumb/Range_center.png/20px-Range_center.png?cbed4" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/en-us/images/thumb/Range_center.png/40px-Range_center.png?cbed4 2x" data-file-width="480" data-file-height="480" /></a></span> <a href="/en-us/Range" title="Range">center range</a></span>. A line of vision cannot extend beyond terrain or an unrevealed brush.<hr style="width:90%; border:solid rgba(255,255,255,0.25); border-width:0px 0px 1px 0px;" />
<p>
<b>Sight</b> can also refer to an effect that makes a unit visible regardless of the fog of war or <span class="glossary" style="white-space:pre; position:relative;" data-game="lol" data-tip="Brush"><span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/en-us/Brush" title="Brush"><img src="/en-us/images/thumb/Ivern_Brushmaker.png/20px-Ivern_Brushmaker.png?4c89b" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/en-us/images/thumb/Ivern_Brushmaker.png/40px-Ivern_Brushmaker.png?4c89b 2x" data-file-width="64" data-file-height="64" /></a></span> <a href="/en-us/Brush" title="Brush">brush</a></span>, allowing them to be targeted. <span class="glossary" style="white-space:pre; <p><b>Invisibility</b> is a form of stealth that renders a unit hidden from enemy <span class="glossary" style="white-space:pre; position:relative;" data-game="lol" data-tip="Sight"><span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/en-us/Sight" title="Sight"><img src="/en-us/images/thumb/Sight_icon.png/20px-Sight_icon.png?0aaf0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/en-us/images/Sight_icon.png?0aaf0 2x" data-file-width="26" data-file-height="26" /></a></span> <a href="/en-us/Sight" title="Sight">vision</a></span> for a <i>short</i> period of time.
</p>
<ul><li><b>Invisible</b> units become visible <b>only</b> when affected by <span class="glossary" style="white-space:pre; position:relative;" data-game="lol" data-tip="True sight"><span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/en-us/Sight#True_sight" title="Sight"><img src="/en-us/images/thumb/True_Sight_icon.png/20px-True_Sight_icon.png?8830f" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/en-us/images/thumb/True_Sight_icon.png/40px-True_Sight_icon.png?8830f 2x" data-file-width="128" data-file-height="128" /></a></span> <a href="/en-us/Sight#True_sight" title="Sight">True Sight</a></span>.<hr style="width:90%; border:solid rgba(255,255,255,0.25); border-width:0px 0px 1px 0px;" /></li></ul>
<p>
A <i>stealthed</i> unit's character model briefly shimmers for every instance of damage taken while <i>stealthed</i>. This shimmering does not make the unit <a href="/en-us/Unit_selection" title="Unit selection">selectable</a> nor remove the <i>stealth</i> effect, only compromises its position.</p>
